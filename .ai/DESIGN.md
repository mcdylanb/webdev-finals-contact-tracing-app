Sentinel Access Design System

1. Visual Language & Aesthetic

The "Sentinel Access" aesthetic is defined by functional minimalism. It prioritizes high-contrast readability and generous whitespace to ensure a professional, secure, and user-friendly experience in high-traffic environments.





Primary Metaphor: Digital Gateway.



Key Principles: Clarity, Trust, Efficiency, Minimalism.

2. Color Palette

The palette uses deep architectural blues against a backdrop of clean grays and pure whites to establish a sense of institutional security and modern technology.

3. Typography

The system uses Inter (or equivalent high-legibility sans-serif) to maintain a modern, neutral tone.





Headlines: Semi-bold to Bold, tight tracking, high contrast (e.g., text-4xl for welcome screens).



Subheadings: Medium weight, generous leading for readability.



Body: Regular weight, text-base for standard information.



Labels: Semi-bold, text-sm, uppercase for secondary metadata (e.g., form field headers).

4. Components & Patterns

Cards & Containers





Elevation: Flat with subtle borders (border-outline-variant) or very soft shadows (shadow-sm) to define depth without clutter.



Corner Radius: ROUND_EIGHT (8px) for a balanced, modern feel that isn't too playful or too sharp.

Navigation (Kiosk Pattern)





Top Bar: Minimalist, fixed to the top, containing brand logo and status indicators.



Footer: Reserved for system status, versioning, or essential secondary actions like "Admin Login".

Inputs & Forms





Design: Large touch targets for kiosks, clear labeling above the field.



Focus State: 2px primary color border with subtle outer glow.



Validation: Minimalist inline feedback; no heavy error banners unless critical.

Buttons





Primary: Solid blue background, white text, bold label. High-intent actions (e.g., "Verify Information").



Secondary: Outlined or ghost buttons for lower-intent actions (e.g., "Go Back", "Cancel").

5. Interaction States





Hover: Subtle background darkening or elevation increase (desktop only).



Active/Tap: 95% scale-down to provide tactile feedback on touchscreens.



Success States: Large, centered icons with smooth entry animations to signal completion clearly.

6. Iconography





Style: Linear (Outlined), consistent stroke weight (2px), primary color usage for emphasis.



Library: Lucide or Material Symbols (Sharp/Rounded).
